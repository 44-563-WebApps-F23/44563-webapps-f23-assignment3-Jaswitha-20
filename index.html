My Favourite Vacation Spot is Bangalore

Bangalore is located in Karnataka state, India. It is also known as Electronic city of India. I like Bangalore because of it's climatic conditions and there will be people from different cultures. It is also popular for IT hub and Startups. There are many tourist attractions near the city and many adventure sports in the city.

My Favourite activities to do at my Vacation spot

  1. Playing adventure sports in Wonderla and Funland.
  2. Visiting nearby waterfalls and doing kayaking
  3. Other sports like Bowling, GoKarting etc..
