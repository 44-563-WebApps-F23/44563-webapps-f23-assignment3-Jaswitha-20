Kristoff Journalist

Kristoff is a well known journalist in th city, who strictly follows law and order. He is praised by citizens of his city because of his sincerity and wisdom.
He often has to involve in many issues but without frightening he always reveals the truth to the society.

Traits of my Fictional Character
